Study of tools - 1
-------------------

- by rule, groups of 2-3 students
- prepare slides and demo code
- 15 min. presentation + 10 min. demo & questions
- each group presents a different tool

- some evaluation criteria:
- - coverage (breadth, depth)
- - highlight of main functionalities
- - clarity, pedagogical quality
- - a good balance of the previous points for the slot of 15+10 min.

Suggestion: start by understanding the selected tool/API and build
a demo; then prepare the presentation to provide context and explain
the demo.

Submit presentation (pdf) and demo code at: https://classroom.github.com/a/5TC3BmdK
As group name, use your student numbers, e.g. 111111_222222_333333
Deadline: 2025/03/12, 24h

More than evaluation moments, the STs are learning moments, so: do your best and relax :-)
